About The Team

Algonquin College Rocketry (ACR) is not just a team; we are a mission-driven collective Igniting the Arc of Innovation to Soar Among the Stars! (ACR) is a multidisciplinary team that aims to design and build a rocket that will reach Mach 2 and 22,000 feet. Our team is constantly improving our skills in various fields such as mechanical, electrical, robotics, and computer engineering, as well as business, marketing, and graphic design.

Launch Canada Competition 2024

ACR’s mission is to create the best rockets possible, showing our skills and innovation while improving our team’s abilities and representing Algonquin College.

We’re competing in the Basic Category of the 2024 Launch Canada Competition against Universities across Canada. The Basic Category limits design to exclusively commercial off-the-shelf parts (COTS). In the future, we plan to participate in Launch Canada’s Advanced Category and the Spaceport American Cup.
