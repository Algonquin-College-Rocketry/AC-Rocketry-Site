About the Team

The Algonquin Rocketry Team is a dynamic and passionate group of students, comprised of diverse talents from various disciplines within Algonquin College in Ottawa. Driven by innovation and a relentless pursuit of excellence, the Algonquin Rocketry Team is committed to designing, constructing, and launching a cutting-edge rocket that not only meets, but surpasses industry standards. Fuelled by creativity and propelled by teamwork, their mission extends beyond competition; it's about fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and hands-on experience in aerospace engineering.

The team welcomes collaboration, partnerships, and support from industry leaders, sponsors, and fellow enthusiasts who share their zeal for exploration and technological advancement in aerospace engineering. Together, they aim to push the boundaries of innovation and redefine what's possible in rocketry.

Launch Canada Competition 2024

The Launch Canada competition stands as a catalyst for propelling Canada's aerospace and rocketry disciplines forward. Focused on fostering scientific, engineering, and business advancements within these industries, Launch Canada champions hands-on learning experiences for students.

This initiative unites Canada's burgeoning student rocketry community, bridging academia, industry, and government sectors. Through collaboration and synergy, Launch Canada aims to unleash students' innovative capabilities.
